Theorem 1.2.

For any open sentence \(P (x)\text{,}\)

\begin{align*} \neg \left( \forall x \in U \right) [P(x)] \amp \equiv \left( \exists x \in U \right) [\neg P(x)], \text{ and }\\ \neg \left( \exists x \in U \right) [P(x)] \amp \equiv \left( \forall x \in U \right) [\neg P(x)]\text{.} \end{align*}
